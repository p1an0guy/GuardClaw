# Product Steering

GuardClaw is a consent-based household safety coordinator for families and trusted caregivers.

The MVP focuses on one demoable flow: replay a nearby public safety alert, combine it with household context, identify who is most affected, and produce a calm action plan with outbound message drafts.

## Product Principles
- Consent first: household context is explicitly provided for the demo.
- Calm over alarmist: explain risk and actions in plain language.
- Coordination, not command: GuardClaw suggests next steps but does not impersonate authorities.
- Demo clarity: simulated/replay data must be visible in the UI and API.
- Privacy by default: no real camera stream, no hidden monitoring, no real outbound sends in MVP.
